<p><strong>The Refractories Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaped Refractories</li><li>Unshaped Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refractories market is categorized into two main types: shaped and unshaped refractories. Shaped refractories are prefabricated forms, like bricks and tiles, designed for specific applications in high-temperature environments, ensuring structural integrity and thermal resistance. In contrast, unshaped refractories consist of pliable mixtures, such as castables and ramming masses, that can be poured or shaped on-site. Both types are crucial in industries like metal, glass, and ceramics, providing essential heat resistance and durability in demanding conditions.</p></p>
