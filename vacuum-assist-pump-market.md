<p><strong>The Vacuum Assist Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prime Pump</li><li>Dry-Prime Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum assist pump market includes two primary types: prime pumps and dry-prime pumps. Prime pumps utilize a wet priming method, where they are initially filled with fluid to create suction, making them ideal for handling liquids with a low level of solids. In contrast, dry-prime pumps are equipped with a vacuum system that removes air from the pump casing before operation, allowing them to handle diverse fluids, including those with higher solid content, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
