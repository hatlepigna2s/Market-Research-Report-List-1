Low power relays handle slightly higher currents, often used in consumer electronics. Medium power relays cater to industrial applications, supporting moderate voltage and currents. High power relays are built for heavy-duty tasks, managing substantial loads in industrial automation and electrical systems. Each type serves distinct applications tailored to specific electrical demands.</p></p>
