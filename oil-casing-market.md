<p><strong>The Oil Casing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Casing</li><li>Technical Casing</li><li>Oil Layer Casing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil casing market comprises three main types: Surface Casing, Technical Casing, and Oil Layer Casing. Surface Casing is the first layer that secures the well and protects groundwater, ensuring stability at shallow depths. Technical Casing is used for deeper sections, providing structural integrity and preventing formation collapse. Oil Layer Casing is designed specifically for optimal production, isolating the oil reservoir from other layers and enhancing extraction efficiency. Each type plays a critical role in maintaining well integrity and maximizing production.</p></p>
