<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Motor</li><li>AC Induction Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle (NEV) traction motors market primarily features two types: Permanent Magnet Motors and AC Induction Motors. Permanent Magnet Motors utilize permanent magnets to create a strong magnetic field, offering high efficiency and compact design, making them ideal for electric vehicles. AC Induction Motors, on the other hand, operate using electromagnetic induction, providing robustness and lower costs, but often with reduced efficiency compared to permanent magnet options. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing NEV performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
