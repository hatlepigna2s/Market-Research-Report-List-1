<p><p>The hydraulic retarders market can be categorized into three main types: single-stage, dual-stage, and multi-stage retarders. Single-stage retarders use a single hydraulic unit to provide braking force, making them simple and effective for various applications. Dual-stage retarders incorporate two hydraulic systems for enhanced performance and braking efficiency, suitable for heavier vehicles. Multi-stage retarders utilize multiple hydraulic stages to offer superior braking capabilities, ensuring optimal control and heat dissipation, making them ideal for high-demand environments such as heavy-duty trucks and buses.</p></p>
