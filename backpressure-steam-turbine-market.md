<p><p>Backpressure steam turbines are categorized into two main types: single-stage and multistage. </p><p>Single-stage turbines utilize one rotor and are typically used for low-pressure applications, providing a simpler design and lower cost. Multistage turbines, on the other hand, consist of multiple rotor stages, enabling efficient handling of high-pressure steam and offering greater power output and adaptability to varying load conditions. Both types are crucial for optimizing energy recovery and improving overall plant efficiency in various industrial applications.</p></p>
