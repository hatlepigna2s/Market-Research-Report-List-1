<p><strong>The PET Bottle Filling Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Without Contact Valve</li><li>With Contact Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET bottle filling machines market is divided into two main types: without contact valve and with contact valve. Machines without contact valves fill bottles using a gravity or pressure-based system, minimizing contamination risk by eliminating direct contact with the liquid. In contrast, machines with contact valves ensure precise filling by making direct contact with the bottle opening, enhancing accuracy. Both types cater to various production needs, offering solutions for different levels of sanitation, speed, and operational efficiency in bottling lines.</p></p>
