VHF antennas are commonly used for public safety and emergency services due to their longer range, suitable for rural areas. UHF antennas offer better penetration in urban environments, making them ideal for commercial and industrial communication. SHF antennas are used for specialized applications, including broadband communications, providing high data rates in increasingly dense urban settings.</p></p>
