<p><p>Projection paint is categorized into two main types: white screen paint and gray screen paint. White screen paint is designed to reflect maximum brightness, making it ideal for well-lit environments where vibrant colors and sharp details are essential. In contrast, gray screen paint enhances contrast and black levels, reducing ambient light's impact, which is beneficial in darker rooms. Both types serve specific needs depending on the viewing conditions, offering users tailored solutions for optimal projection experiences.</p></p>
