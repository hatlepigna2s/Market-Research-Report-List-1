<p><p>Embedded hardware for Edge AI encompasses various types, including GPUs, VPUs, FPGAs, and ASICs. GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) excel in parallel processing, making them suitable for complex AI tasks. VPUs (Vision Processing Units) are optimized for low-power vision applications, enhancing efficiency in edge devices. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) provide flexibility, allowing for custom configurations tailored to specific workloads. ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) offer high performance and energy efficiency by being designed for particular applications, making them ideal for mass-produced AI solutions.</p></p>
