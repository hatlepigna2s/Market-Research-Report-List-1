<p><p>The Transient Voltage Suppression (TVS) diode market is primarily divided into two types: uni-polar and bi-polar TVS diodes. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed to protect circuits by clamping positive voltage transients, making them suitable for single-polarity applications. In contrast, bi-polar TVS diodes can handle both positive and negative transients, offering broader protection for bidirectional signals. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing device reliability in environments prone to voltage spikes while safeguarding sensitive electronic components.</p></p>
