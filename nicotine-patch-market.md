<p><p>The nicotine patch market is primarily divided into two types: 16-hour patches and 24-hour patches. The 16-hour patches are designed for daytime use, providing nicotine release for the duration of waking hours, ideal for users looking for a specific cessation support. Each type caters to different preferences and smoking cessation needs.</p></p>
