<p><p>The pharmaceutical grade PVDC market comprises two primary types: PVDC resins and PVDC latex. PVDC resins are solid materials used in packaging applications, known for their excellent barrier properties, protecting pharmaceuticals from moisture and oxygen. In contrast, PVDC latex is a water-based dispersion used in coatings and films, offering flexibility and easier application. Both types are essential in ensuring the stability and integrity of pharmaceutical products, enhancing their shelf life and safety for consumers.</p></p>
