<p><p>The high viscosity pumps market comprises three main types: positive-displacement pumps, centrifugal pumps, and other specialized pumps. Positive-displacement pumps, designed to handle thick fluids, move a fixed volume with each cycle, making them ideal for viscous materials. Centrifugal pumps use rotational energy to transport fluids but may struggle with high-viscosity substances. Other market types include gear pumps, diaphragm pumps, and progressive cavity pumps, each catering to specific applications in industries like oil, food, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
