<p><strong>The Textile Leveling Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fibrophilic Leveling Agents</li><li>Dye-Philic Leveling Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Textile leveling agents are essential additives used to ensure uniform dye application on fabrics. Fibrophilic leveling agents primarily interact with textile fibers, facilitating the even distribution of dye by enhancing the affinity between the dye and the fiber surface. In contrast, dye-philic leveling agents focus on promoting the solubility and dispersion of dyes within the dye bath, reducing the likelihood of uneven coloration. Both types optimize dye uptake, improve fabric appearance, and enhance overall textile quality.</p></p>
