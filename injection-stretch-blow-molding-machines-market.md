<p><ul><li>Single-Stage Type</li><li>Two-Stage Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Injection stretch blow molding machines are categorized into two main types: single-stage and two-stage. The single-stage type integrates injection molding and blow molding processes in one machine, allowing for efficient production of preforms and their immediate conversion to finished products. In contrast, the two-stage type separates these processes, first producing preforms through injection and then forming the final product in a separate blow molding stage. Each type offers distinct advantages in terms of production speed, cost-effectiveness, and flexibility, catering to different manufacturing needs.</p></p>
