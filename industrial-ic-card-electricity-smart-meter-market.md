<p><p>The industrial IC card electricity smart meter market is categorized into single-phase and three-phase meter types. Single-phase smart meters are designed for residential or small commercial use, measuring electricity consumption through a single alternating current (AC) phase. In contrast, three-phase smart meters cater to industrial applications and larger commercial facilities, measuring power consumption across three AC phases. Both types utilize integrated circuit (IC) technology for accurate billing and enhanced energy management, catering to varying consumer needs and power load requirements.</p></p>
