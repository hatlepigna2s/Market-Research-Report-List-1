<p><p>The magnetic speed sensor market is divided into two primary types: active and passive magnetic speed sensors. Active magnetic speed sensors generate their own electromagnetic field and provide a continuous output signal, making them ideal for High-speed operations. In contrast, passive magnetic speed sensors rely on external magnetic fields to operate, offering simpler construction and lower cost. While active sensors deliver precise measurements in demanding environments, passive sensors are commonly used in less complex applications, balancing efficiency and affordability.</p></p>
