<p><p>The automotive steering shaft market comprises several types, including splined shafts, double D shafts, and others. Splined shafts feature grooves that provide a secure connection between components, allowing for rotational movement while maintaining alignment. Double D shafts have a flat, D-shaped profile, ensuring a tight fit and reducing slippage. The "others" category encompasses various specialized shafts designed for unique vehicle requirements. Each type plays a critical role in steering response, vehicle handling, and overall safety in automotive applications.</p></p>
