<p><strong>The Marine Fender System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid Rubber Fenders</li><li>Pneumatic Fenders</li><li>Foam Fenders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine fender system market includes three primary types: solid rubber fenders, pneumatic fenders, and foam fenders. Solid rubber fenders are durable and provide excellent energy absorption for ship-to-berth interactions. Pneumatic fenders use compressed air for buoyancy and impact cushioning, making them suitable for larger vessels and harsher conditions. Foam fenders are lightweight and offer good energy absorption with minimal water absorption, making them ideal for limited draft areas. Each type serves distinct applications based on vessel size and environmental conditions.</p></p>
