<p><strong>The Indirect Acting Solenoid Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two-Way Solenoid Valve</li><li>Three-Way Solenoid Valve</li><li>Four-Way Solenoid Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The indirect acting solenoid valve market includes three main types: two-way, three-way, and four-way solenoid valves. Two-way valves control the flow in a single direction and are used in applications like water and gas flow regulation. Three-way valves manage the flow between two outlets, allowing for mixing or diverting of fluids. Four-way valves control the flow between two inlets and two outlets, typically used in applications like pneumatic actuators to alternate the flow direction.</p></p>
