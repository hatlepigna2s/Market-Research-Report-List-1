<p><p>The ferrite cores market is categorized into Nickel-Zinc (Ni-Zn) ferrite cores, Mn-Zn ferrite cores, and others. Nickel-Zinc ferrite cores are primarily used in high-frequency applications, such as RF inductors and transformers, due to their high impedance at elevated frequencies. Mn-Zn ferrite cores are favored for lower frequency applications like power transformers and inductors, given their high saturation magnetization. The "others" category may include various specialized ferrite materials used in niche applications, providing diverse options for manufacturers and industries.</p></p>
