<p><p>The medical hemostatic agents market comprises various types, including gelation hemostats, combination hemostats, and others. Gelation hemostats, such as gelatin-based products, promote clotting by enhancing the natural coagulation process. Combination hemostats incorporate multiple agents, such as thrombin and fibrinogen, to provide synergistic effects for faster hemostasis. Other hemostatic agents include topical agents and powders that facilitate blood clotting through diverse mechanisms. Together, these products are essential for managing bleeding in surgical and trauma settings.</p></p>
