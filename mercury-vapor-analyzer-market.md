<p><strong>The Mercury Vapor Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption</li><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Fluorescence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mercury Vapor Analyzer market includes two primary types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) and Cold Vapor Atomic Fluorescence (CVAF). CVAA employs a cold vapor technique to detect mercury concentrations by measuring absorbance of light. Conversely, CVAF utilizes fluorescence emitted from mercury vapor when excited by a specific wavelength of light, providing sensitivity and selectivity. Both methods are essential for environmental monitoring and industrial applications, ensuring compliance with safety regulations concerning mercury emissions.</p></p>
