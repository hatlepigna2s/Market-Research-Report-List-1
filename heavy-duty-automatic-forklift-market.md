Fork forklifts are designed for heavy lifting and precise maneuverability in various industrial settings, ideal for transporting goods directly on pallets. In contrast, piggyback forklifts combine a standard lift truck with an additional attachment that allows for transportation over rough terrain, enhancing versatility. Both types facilitate efficient material handling, improve safety, and streamline operations in warehouses and construction sites.</p></p>
